I am Xiong Shi (石雄), a global change ecologist. I obtained my bachelor’s degree from Shaanxi Normal University. I then continued my master's research at the Xishuangbanna Tropical Botanical Garden (XTBG), Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Subsequently, I earned my PhD from the University of Konstanz in Germany, supervised by Prof. Dr. Mark van Kleunen. Starting May 2026, I will join Peking University as a Postdoctoral Fellow, where my research will continue to focus on plant ecology and the complex interactions of accumulating global change drivers.
